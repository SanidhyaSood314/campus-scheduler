How it Works
1. Fill out and submit your request.
2. Booking is marked Pending immediately.
3. Admin Approves or Rejects it.
4. Approved bookings are locked confirmed slots.
Booking Rules
• Bookings must be for future dates only.
• Overlapping approved slots are automatically blocked.
• You can edit a Pending booking before it's reviewed.
• Editing resets status to Pending for re-review.
• Only admins can delete bookings.
